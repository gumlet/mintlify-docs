Below are the steps to upload videos and start streaming:

### 1. Upload Video

You can upload videos to a collection by clicking on `New Video` button. You choose files from your device. You can also import from other storage providers such as OneDrive, Dropbox, Google Drive, or a URL.

<Frame caption="Video Upload Box">
  <img src="/images/docs/6d7d4d16bac2379767180a349fd0514daabaca9bed5cfb504c907d2fc69ada40-Screenshot_2024-09-09_at_8.35.04_PM.png" />
</Frame>

> You can also drag and drop videos directly inside this box for quick upload

### 2. Start Streaming

Gumlet makes processing your videos extremely fast so videos are steam-ready within less than 5 seconds!

You can click on the video, go to the video details page, and click on the **Embed & Share** button to get different options to start streaming.
